# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
WeftEnd is an operator-facing tool. We keep discussion factual, respectful, and focused on improving the software and its documentation.

## Expected behavior
- Be professional and constructive.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Assume good intent and avoid personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
- Threats, doxxing, or sharing private information.
- Repeatedly derailing or spamming discussions.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content, restrict access, or block participation for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting
Report concerns to: WeftEnd@aol.com
